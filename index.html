Early College High Computer Club

Background

Welcome to Early College High School's Computer Club website! Since our start in 2015, Early College High (ECH) has been developing more and more every year. From isolation at Northside, to a now new computer club! The computer club had been developed in 2016.

Goals

Our goal in Early College's computer club is to work with computers and design websites. We also learn about the commputer's system and how it works. Our main objective in the computer club is to have an idea about how a computer works, how to fix its problems, and what its purpose is. We also learn about computer programs that help computers operate and display a website's information. Titles such as:

  1. HTML
  2. CSS
  3. JavaScript
  4. Ruby
  5. Rails
